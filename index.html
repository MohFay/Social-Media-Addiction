Social Media Addiction

a behavioral disorder characterized by excessive,
compulsive use of social media platforms that
negatively impacts daily life, relationships, and mental health.

Fake Persona ↴

People post fake images of them looking happy when they are not.

Teens spend so much time online they forget about real world conversation face to face.

Teens stay up scrolling all night and makes poor sleep and not focused in real life.
